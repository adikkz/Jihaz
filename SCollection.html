Find your sound

Home is the new studio! Created together with electronic music artists, Kazakh House Mafia, this just-launched collection helps music makers and other creatives to find their state of flow, wherever they call home.

Almost every musician – even Kazakh House Mafia – started out at home. Maybe with just a guitar in the garage, a voice in front of the mirror, or on a laptop in the living room. With the new SHEKTEULERSIZ collection, on sale around the world from 1 October, it has never been simpler to make room for creativity in the space you already have.

Unlimited creativity for those with limited means
